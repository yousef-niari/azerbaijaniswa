About AWSA

AWSA brings together Azerbaijani families, students, professionals, and friends across Washington State. We celebrate Azerbaijani heritage, build community connections, and support cultural understanding through education and public events.

Our Mission

AWSA is dedicated to promoting and preserving Azerbaijani culture and language. We aim to foster a strong sense of community identity by celebrating our rich heritage and creating opportunities for our community to connect, learn, and share traditions. We organize cultural events to showcase our traditions, fostering unity and shared identity. Our initiatives promote inclusivity, aiming to create a vibrant environment where our people can flourish. We strive to build bridges between our community and the wider community, fostering mutual understanding and respect for cultural diversity.

Our Vision

We envision a Washington State where Azerbaijani culture and language are not only preserved, but also cherished and celebrated. We aim to foster a community filled with pride in our heritage, where our traditions are passed down through generations. Our goal is to lead in supporting individuals in preserving their unique cultural identity, and to promote intercultural understanding and appreciation among all communities in Washington State. Ultimately, we aspire for a diverse and inclusive society that values and respects all cultural heritages, ensuring Azerbaijani culture thrives as part of Washington’s multicultural fabric.
